FANTA

INGREDIENTS

Fanta orange is a tantalizing carbonated soft drink renowned for its zesty and citrusy flavor profile. At its heart, fanta orange boasts a base of pure carbonated water, which serves as a refreshing canvas for the vibrant orange essence that dominates its taste. To achieve its signature tangy and sweet notes, fanta orange incorporates high fructose corn syrup, providing a balanced sweetness that harmonizes beautifully with the natural orange flavors. Citric acid lends a delightful tartness to the mix, enhancing the overall zestiness. This effervescent beverage is rounded out with a touch of sodium citrate and sodium benzoate, ensuring its freshness and quality. The result is a bright and invigorating concoction that captures the essence of ripe, sun-kissed oranges in every bubbly sip.
